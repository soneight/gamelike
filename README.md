# GAMELIKE
> Resemble Role Playing Games: based of classic games in medieval dark fantasy style

## Games

Each game should support some kind of multiplayer, but can also be played without internet connection

Should contain launcher application to run:

- `Rogue-like`: _progression RPG_; base: **Rogue (1980)**
- `Might-like`: _turn-based strategy RPG like HOMM series;_ base: **Heroes of Might and Magic III (1999)**
- `Craft-like`: _real-time strategy like warcraft series;_ base **Warcraft 3 The Frozen Throne (2003)**
- `Drage-like`: _hybrid RPG like dragon age series;_ base: **Dragon Age Origins (2009)**
- `Risen-like`: _classic RPG like Gothic andor risen series, elder scrolls series;_ base: **Risen (2009)**
- `Exile-like`: _top-down action RPG like Diablo series or path of exile;_ base: **Path of Exile (2013)**
- `Souls-like`: _third-person action RPG like dark souls series;_ base: **Dark Souls 2 (2014)**
- `Magic-like`: _collection card like hearthstone or magic arena;_ base: **Magic The Gathering Arena (2019)**

## Story
> Raimenals (`Reproducible Artificial Intelligence MEN ALike` in plural form) is "next generation" of humans

Era zero is transition period, reason of human disappearance is not fully known and used as `easter eggs` for games from era I to VIII.

Due to probable extinction humans created underworld data centers with information about their restoration process by artificial intelligence. But because of data corruption instead of recreating humans AI build human-alike robots (Raimenals) that believed that they are humans, process of restoring corrupted data never stops. Because of huge amount of corrupted and conflicting information robots start building magical fantasy medieval sword and sorcery world without gunpowder as it could lead to creating mass destruction weapons. All resources that could lead to creation of such weapons are destroyed or used as building blocks of magic-enabler structures and creatures.

Each game placed in different era, earlier era corresponds to earlier OpenGL version and deliberately should look worse than next era

This robotic nature is significantly ease the process of modeling and animating characters

### Key Points

- `AIR` (`Vjuter`): _creation of `intelligent` dragons have unseen consequences of their hostile behavior toward `artificial` robots_
- `ARC` (`Dyga`): _the decision about dragons was to begin process of their domestication in `colonies` and killing the wilder than mostly `ancient` ones_
- `PRO` (`Gyry`): _leading group `organization` discovers the truth of Era Zero, they have the means to bring back humans but choose to keep it `private` to remain the masters of their world_
- `DRY` (`Syho`): _secret leaks, realization that they are not real humans causes a global depression and a `dark` `year`, the robotic unity splits into those who accept their nature and those who hate it_
- `ORB` (`Sfera`): _an attempt to finally use the human data is interrupted by a faction that fears the end of robotic life, this `betrayal` corrupts the human data-stream with `obscure` information_
- `FUR` (`Hytro`): _because of the previous era, the human data manifests as a physical, aggressive biological parasite `fibers` that `usurp` robots like hair or wool, merging metal with synthetic flesh_
- `WAR` (`Vjuina`): _different robotic factions begin battles `among` each other that leads to a great `wildfire`_
- `RED` (`Krov`): _after never stopping battles the world is now a mix of small groups of different entities, robotic hero faces the ultimate choice restoring real humans that leads to `death` of robots, or let the robots continue their `ethereal` existence_
